Speckled Blueberry Lemonade

Glassware: 3D Shaker - No top (Does not come with glass)

Ingredients:

  • 9 oz. Lemonade
  • 1 oz. Sliced Blueberry

    • Procedure

    Fill margarita shaker with ice.

    Measure in all sliced strawberries on top of ice.

    Fill with lemonade.

    Garnish:
