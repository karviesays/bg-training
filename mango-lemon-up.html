Mango Lemon Up

Glassware: 16 oz. Projector Lite Up (Comes with glass)

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz. Lemonade
  • 1 oz. Sweet & Sour
  • 4 oz. 7 Up
  • 1 oz. Mango Puree

    • Procedure

    Method (By Hand)

    In a blender, measure all ingredients

    Add One level scoop of ice, cover and blend.

    Pour blended mixture into Projector Glass.

    Method (Lemon Up Machine)

    In a Projector glass, measure in blue sugar syrup.

    Fill with Lemon up mix from machine.

    Top Projector glass with a plastic tumbler and shake vigorously to mix.

    Garnish:

    Long Straw
