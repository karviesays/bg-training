Forget Me Not (Blueberry Smoothie)

Glassware: 16oz. Pint Glass (Does not come with glass)

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz. Vanilla Ice Cream
  • 2 oz. Blueberry Toppings
  • 2 oz. Sliced Strawberries
  • 4 oz. Milk

    • Procedure

    Add all ingredients into a blender.

    Blend until thick and all ingredients are mixed well together. Pour into 16oz. Pint glass.

    Use a spoon to get remaining smoothie out of the blender cup.

    Garnish:

    Long Tea Spoon & Medium Straw
